How to Use?

Step 1

Enter Your Name
  • Begin by entering your username to personalize your Medix experience.
  • A unique profile is created for you.
  • Your diagnosis history is stored in this profile.
  • Medix provides tailored insights based on past interactions.

Step 2

Input Your Symptoms
  • Enter your symptoms in a comma-separated list.
  • Example: "fever, cough, fatigue".
  • Provide as much detail as possible.
  • This helps Medix analyze your condition accurately.

Step 3

Review Diagnosis
  • Medix analyzes your symptoms to suggest a possible diagnosis.
  • Example: symptoms such as fever and cough can be assessed.
  • Suggestions provided are informational in nature.
  • Always consult a licensed physician to confirm the diagnosis.

Step 4

View Report & Prescription
  • Get a report of symptoms, diagnosis, and history.
  • Includes sample meds (e.g., "Ibuprofen 200mg for flu").
  • Review carefully and consult a doctor first.
  • Suggestions are for emergencies or reference only.
